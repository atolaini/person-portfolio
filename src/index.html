About Me...

Thanks for taking the time to take a look at my site. As you may have already guessed my name is Adam (The long version is Adam Tolaini), and I'm a web developer based in London.

After years working in the finance industry I decided to change paths and took what was a hobby and try and turn it into a career. Since leaving the finance industy I have been learning and developing the skills needed to be a modern day developer.

Currently I specialize in front end development using the latest web technologies to create modern looking websites and web applications. I have worked on freelance projects building and in some cases designing modern responsive websites.

I am always eager to learn more and expand my skills and currently looking for any junior postions or freelance work.
